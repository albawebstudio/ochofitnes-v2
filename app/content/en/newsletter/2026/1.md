### A Note From Me

I had the fortune of having my kids in Mexico for 30 days. Sometimes it felt like I was back home, like everything was
back to normal. It truly was an incredible time with the kids. My heart was so happy to see them come off the plane, and
my heart was broken seeing them leave. People don’t always realize how much a child can suffer in situations like this.

I’m going to miss bedtimes. Holding their hands everywhere we walked. Listening to their stories. Taking care of them
when they got sick. Making dinners for them. It’s hard to know what to say when you hear your child ask you when you’ll
be home. I can go through hard things, but seeing a kid suffer hits differently. It’s a unique feeling seeing your kids
board a plane knowing you can’t come home with them.

So now, it’s just me again. Only today I feel stronger. That’s a good place for all of us to be, stronger mentally and
physically. Stay savage!
